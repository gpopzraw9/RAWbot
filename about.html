About GpopzRaw

Meet Haroon Ahmad Amin — a self-taught developer, educator, and builder of digital dreams.

My Journey

I started my journey with a simple curiosity about how things work online. From building small websites to diving deep into AI, cybersecurity, and education, I've always believed that learning by doing is the best way forward.

Over the years, I’ve built tools, created tutorials, spoken at events, and helped others learn technology through hands-on projects. GpopzRaw is where all my experiences, ideas, and creations come together.
